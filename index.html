About Radio Damroo

Radio Damroo is the finest and most popular digital radio station in Etawah city, Uttar Pradesh, India. Our mission is to keep you connected with pure non-stop high-quality music, local happenings, and clean family-friendly entertainment. All of our talented RJs are live with unique show concepts, interactive games, and superhit playlists.

Through our advanced web platform and Android applications, we deliver a premium, crystal-clear audio broadcasting experience in digital media. When Damroo plays, the entertainment never stops! Stay tuned for non-stop blasting hits from your favorite local station.
